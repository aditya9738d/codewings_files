How to Get a TestFlight Invitation Code

By following these detailed steps, you should be able to obtain a TestFlight invitation code and join the beta program successfully.

Step 1 - Navigate to the Beta Program Website:

  • Open your Telegram and go to the channel CodeWings that offers the beta program you are interested in.

Step 2 - Find the "Join the Beta" Button:

  • Locate a button or a link that says "Join the Beta." This might be highlighted or prominently placed to attract beta testers.
  • Click on the "Join the Beta" button or link.
