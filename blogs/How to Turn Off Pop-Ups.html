How to Turn Off Pop-Ups



Turn off pop-ups for a better browsing experience.

  • In Chrome, go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Pop-ups and redirects. Turn off Allowed.
  • In Safari, go to Preferences > Websites > Pop-up Windows. Block pop-ups from websites.
  • In Firefox, go to Options > Privacy & Security > Permissions. Uncheck Block pop-up windows.
